Frequently Asked Questions

After having worked with lots of prospective and current clients, I stated noticing some questions which surface again and again. Below I've listed the most common questions for your convenience, but if you have any questions please feel free to get in touch and I would be more than happy to help.

What do I need to start building my website?

A)Domain Name - A domain name is what people will enter in the browser address bar to find your website(for example: http://stuartgreen.me.uk/ is my domain name). The most commonly used extension for domains is .com, but there are many others (.net, .org, .gov, .edu, .tv, etc).

B)Web hosting - Web hosting is like real estate for your website. This is where all of your websites files are stored and hosted.

C)Professional Web Designer - Trust me, you dont want your brother-in-law designing your website unless he does it professionally. Youre at the right lace and in the right hands here, I hope we can get started soon!

Its worth noting that I can provide all of the above services, should you want me to purchase a domain name for you and host your files on my server, hosting starts from just 20 per year.
